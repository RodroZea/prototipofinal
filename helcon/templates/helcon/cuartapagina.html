Health Benefits of Sleep

Sleeping well is essential for our health and emotional well-being.

Getting enough sleep and having good quality sleep are essential for healthy sleep.

The amount of sleep you need changes as you age.

Talk to your healthcare provider if you have trouble sleeping.

The importance of the preventive care

Preventive care is a comprehensive health approach that aims to guarantee the well-being of individuals throughout their entire life, from health promotion and disease prevention to treatment, rehabilitation, and palliative care.

Its purpose is to ensure an equitable distribution of health, providing care centered on the needs of individuals as close as possible to their everyday environment.
